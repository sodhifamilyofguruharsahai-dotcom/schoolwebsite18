Our School Facilities

School Bus

Safe and convenient transport for students. A school bus provides safe and reliable transportation for students to and from school every day. It follows fixed routes and ensures comfort, discipline, and timely travel for children.

Computer Lab

Modern computers for practical learning. The computer lab helps students learn modern technology and develop essential digital skills. It provides access to computers, internet, and educational software for practical learning.

Green Play Ground

The school playground provides a space for students to play, exercise, and stay physically active. It helps in developing teamwork, sportsmanship, and discipline among students. The playground also supports students’ physical health and overall development.
